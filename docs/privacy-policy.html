Privacy Policy

My Neatbook respects your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled.

Data Collection

My Neatbook does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data you create (journal entries, chapters, notes) is stored exclusively on your device in a local JSON file at:

  • %LOCALAPPDATA%\MyNeatbook\data.json (Windows)

No data is ever sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Third-Party Services

The app has a link to Ko-fi (https://ko-fi.com) for optional support. Clicking it opens your default browser. No data is shared with Ko-fi by the app itself.

Analytics & Telemetry

My Neatbook contains no analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or usage tracking of any kind.

Internet Access

The app operates fully offline. Internet access is used only when you click the Ko-fi link to open your browser.

Your Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete the data file at any time to remove all information created within the app.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published here.
